Sweet Sneaky Heat Sauce and Marinade

Back in the 80's I did my first pig roast and created a new sauce to go with it - Sweet Sneaky Heat. It's starts off sweet and the heat quietly sneaks up on you without masking the flavor of everything or anything under it.

All Natural Ingredients:

Ketchup (Tomato Concentrate made from Red Ripe Tomatoes, Distilled Vinegar, Sugar, Salt, Onion Powder, Spices, Natural Flavoring), Molasses, White Vinegar, Sugar, Cayenne Pepper, Worcestershire Sauce (Distilled White Vinegar, Molasses, Sugar, Water, Salt, Onions, Anchovies, Garlic, Cloves, Tamarind Extract, Natural Flavorings, Chili Pepper Extract. Contains Anchovies), Onion Powder, Black Pepper, Garlic Powder.

Allergen: Anchovies
